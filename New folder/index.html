Large-scale projects requiring full EIA. Mining, thermal power, large infrastructure.

Scoping required. Cement plants, small thermal, smaller mining operations.

📋

Category B2 – Small Projects

No scoping required. Small industries, irrigation, service sector projects.

Sector-Specific Parameters — Chhattisgarh

⛏️ Mining (Coal / Iron Ore)
  • ✓Mining lease area (ha) — mandatory for Cat A
  • ✓Production capacity (MTPA)
  • ✓Overburden management & mine closure plan
  • ✓Dust suppression measures
  • ⚠Hydrology impact study (Zone III districts)
🏭 Steel / Thermal Power
  • ✓Total project area (ha)
  • ✓Water requirement (MLD) — CG river basin
  • ✓Air quality baseline data
  • ✓Ash pond / slag disposal plan
  • ⚠Forest land diversion (FCA 1980)
